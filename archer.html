THE ARCHER

The Master of Bow and Arrows

Focused Attributes: Dexterity and Wisdom

While the Warrior and Gladiator both enjoy the closeness of war and battle, the Archer is more removed in their pursuit of victory. The Archer has focused all their time in their mastery of Archery.
The Archer is a combat focused class.

Common Weapons: Bow and Arrow

Class Skills: Craft, Jump, Listen, Move Silently, Search, Slight of Hand, and Tumble

Skill Points per level: 4 + INT modifier (*4 if first level)

Hit Die: d8

Class Bonuses:
Dexterity: +1
Constitution: -1
