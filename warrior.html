THE WARRIOR

The Versatile Veteran

Focused Attributes: Strength

The Warrior is focused on furthering their strength in battle through versatility. They can use all types of weapons, and can be placed anywhere on the battlefield. Their strengths lie in the physical. They have trained too much to be very social.
The Warrior is a combat focused class.

Common Weapons: Any

Class Skills: Intimidate, Jump, Ride, Swim, and Tumble

Skill Points per level: 2 + INT modifier (*4 if first level)

Hit Die: d10

Class Bonuses:
Charisma, and Wisdom: -1
Strength and Constitution: +1
