THE PHILOSOPHER

The Ponderer of All

Focused Attributes: Intelligence and Wisdom

The Philosopher is the class of the thinkers. The people who do nothing but question everything. Philosophers are more versed than most in most subjects. The constant studying they do of their subjects means they are usually of little use in combat, however.
The Archer is a knowledge focused class.

Common Weapons: None

Class Skills: Bluff, Concentration, Decipher Script, Diplomacy, Gather Information, Knowledge(all except religion), and Sense Motive

Skill Points per level: 8 + INT modifier (*4 if first level)

Hit Die: d6

Class Bonuses:
Dexterity and Strength: -2, Constitution: -1
Intelligence: +1, Charisma and Wisdom: +2
