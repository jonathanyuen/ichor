THE THIEF

The Nimble Pickpocket

Focused Attributes: Dexterity

The Thief, skilled in the art of pickpocketing - is a most mischievous person. If you ever have a shiny object, a thief will always check it out, with or without your permission.

Common Weapons: Dagger, Sling, Twin Daggers

Class Skills: Appraise, Balance, Bluff, Climb, Diplomacy, Disable Device, Disguise, Escape Artist, Forgery, Gather Information, Hide, Intimidate, Jump, Knowledge(local), Listen, Move Silently, Open Lock, Search, Sense Motive, Sleight of hand, Swim, and Tumble

Skill Points per level: 8 + INT modifier (*4 if first level)

Hit Die: d6

Class Bonuses:
Dexterity and Charisma: +2, Intelligence and Wisdom: +1
Strength and Constitution: -3

Starting Feats:
To Be Determined
