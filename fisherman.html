THE FISHERMAN/FARMER

The Skilled Commoner

Focused Attributes: Dexterity and Strength

A commoner, but skilled in their craft, the Fisherman/Farmer is one who can deal damage and heal through the food that they can harvest. They are very worldly, and so have a lot of practical knowledge that their teammates can use.

Common Weapons: Spear or Hoe

Class Skills: Appraise, Concentration, Diplomacy, Handle Animal, Heal, Jump, Knowledge(local), Knowledge (nature), Ride, Survival, Swim, Use Rope, and Fishing

Skill Points per level: 6 + INT modifier (*4 if first level)

Hit Die: d8

Class Bonuses:
Dexterity and Charisma: +1, Strength: +2
Wisdom: -3, Intelligence: -1
