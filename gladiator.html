THE GLADIATOR

The Bloodthirsty Brute

Focused Attributes: Dexterity, Strength and Constitution

The Gladiator lives for battle. While Warriors may fight for honor or for a higher cause, the Gladiator fights for no other reason than to bathe in their enemies' blood.
The Gladiator is a combat focused class.

Common Weapons: Depends

Class Skills: Climb, Craft, Intimidate, Jump, Knowledge(nature), Listen, Survival and Swim

Skill Points per level: 6 + INT modifier (*4 if first level)

Hit Die: d12

Class Bonuses:
Dexterity, Strength, Constitution: +2
Intelligence: -2, Wisdom: -1, Charisma: -3
