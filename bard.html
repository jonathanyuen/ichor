THE BARD

The Artful Performer

Focused Attributes: Dexterity, Wisdom, and Charisma

The player of songs and entertainer, the Bard keeps the morale and spirits of the group high. They always know how to keep things energized.

Common Weapons: none

Class Skills: Appraise, Bluff, Diplomacy, Disguise, Gather Infomation, Hide, Knowledge (all), Perform, Sleight of Hand,

Skill Points per level: 8 + INT modifier (*4 if first level)

Hit Die: d6

Class Bonuses:
Dexterity: +4
Constitution and Strength: -2
