THE PRIEST

The Divine Communicator

Focused Attributes: Wisdom, Charisma, Intelligence

The Priest is most concerned with pleasing their god. They keep the people in the party well aligned with their gods as well. Priests know a lot about the Gods, and the divine history.

Common Weapons: Depends on their patron

Class Skills: Concentration, Decipher Script, Godly Communion, Knowledge(religion), Depends on their patron

Skill Points per level: 2 + INT modifier (*4 if first level)

Hit Die: 1d8

Class Bonuses:
Intelligence, Charisma, and Wisdom: +1
Dexterity, Strength and Constitution: -1
